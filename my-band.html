Meet the artists

Band Bio

Nation In Ruin are a 5-piece Heavy Metal band from the North East of England. They have been a prominent and active part of the Teesside Metal scene for a number of years. Their members consist of musicians who have performed in various local bands within the Metal genre. Guitarists who were drummers, drummers who are now singers etc. Their previous incarnations and musical family ties have been to such bands as Cornerstone, In A Furious Deathbed, Ashes Of Insurrection, Minus Favour and Face The Unknown, to name but a few.

NIR are here to...

  1. Play some kick-ass tracks
  2. Entertain whomever will listen
  3. Enjoy being on stage, performing for you lovely people!

Their collective musical influences include bands such as:

  • Meshuggah
  • Metallica
  • Black Tongue
  • Knocked Loose
  • Lamb Of God
